FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

What is your design process?

My design process typically includes research, wireframing, prototyping, design, and implementation. I believe in an iterative approach with client feedback at each stage to ensure the final product meets your expectations.

How long does a project usually take?

Project timelines vary depending on complexity and scope. A simple website might take 2-4 weeks, while a more complex application could take 2-3 months. I'll provide a detailed timeline during our initial consultation.

What are your payment terms?

I typically require a 50% deposit to begin work, with the remaining 50% due upon project completion. For larger projects, we can arrange a milestone-based payment schedule.

Do you offer maintenance services?

Yes, I offer maintenance packages to keep your website or application running smoothly. These can include regular updates, security patches, and content changes.
